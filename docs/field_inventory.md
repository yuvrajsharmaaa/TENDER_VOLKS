# Target Tender Field Inventory

This inventory documents all business metadata fields that the extraction schemas must support. The fields are designed from the business output requirements backward, not from raw OCR text forward.

---

## Basic Information
- **Tender Name:** Short title or description of the work contract.
- **Tender ID:** Canonical unique registration string.
- **NIT Number:** Notice Inviting Tender identifier.
- **Client:** The agency or government body issuing the notice.
- **Department:** Division or regional office executing the project.
- **Organization:** Parent entity (e.g. Ministry of Railways, Central Bureau of Investigation).

---

## Dates and Timeline
- **Publish Date:** The date the notice was made public.
- **Pre-Bid Meeting Date:** Date and time of the clarification forum.
- **Bid Submission Start Date:** Opening window for biddings.
- **Bid Submission End Date / Deadline:** Last date/time for final submission.
- **Bid Opening Date:** Scheduled time for opening technical/financial bids.

---

## Pricing
- **EMD (Earnest Money Deposit):** Mandatory security deposit (amount in INR or indication of exemption).
- **Tender Fee:** Cost of purchasing the document (amount in INR or indication of exemption).
- **Estimated Cost / Tender Value:** Total value of the work contract.
- **Security Deposit:** Performance guarantee or retention percentage.

---

## Eligibility
- **Technical Experience:** Required years of similar work completion or specific contract sizes.
- **Financial Turnover:** Minimum average annual turnover over the last 3-5 financial years.
- **Certifications Required:** Specific standard certifications (e.g. ISO 9001, BIS, CE).
- **OEM Authorization:** Requirements regarding Manufacturer Authorization Forms (MAF).

---

## Technical Requirements
- **Technical Specifications Summary:** Brief list of required products, standards, or performance tolerances.
- **Required Products & Quantities:** Specific line items and quantities needed.
- **Compliance Schedule:** Statements indicating whether partial compliance is accepted.

---

## Checklist Documents
- **PAN Card Proof:** Requirement for corporate taxpayer registration.
- **GST Registration Certificate:** State-level sales tax compliance proof.
- **Turnover Audited Balance Sheets:** Chartered Accountant certified statements.
- **Experience Certificates:** Completion orders from previous public sector contracts.

---

## Contacts
- **Contact Person:** Name or designation of the tender handling officer.
- **Email:** Primary inquiry email address.
- **Phone:** Contact number/extension.
- **Address:** Office or venue location.

---

## Delivery and Address
- **Work/Delivery Location:** Specific site where work is to be executed.
- **Physical Submission Address:** Location for hardcopy submissions.

---

## Risk / Commercial Terms
- **Liquidated Damages (LD) Percentage:** Rate of penalty per week for delays.
- **Maximum LD Cap:** Ceiling on total delay penalties (typically 10% of contract value).
- **Warranty Period:** Mandatory maintenance coverage duration.
- **Blacklisting Clauses:** Non-compliance thresholds resulting in suspension from bidding.
